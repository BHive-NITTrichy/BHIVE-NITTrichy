WHO WE ARE

We are a bunch of civil aspirants intent on providing on-the-job training and proficiency to the multitude of students who want to pursue a career in Civil Engineering. We provide opportunities to perceive, learn, and employ your knowledge towards creating sustainable solutions to modern predicaments.

WHAT WE DO

Profiles!


CONSTRUCTION

We collaborate with construction firms to expose the learners to various aspects of design, construction, and project management and provide guidance and counseling from accomplished professionals.

STRUCTURAL

We work on projects related to structural engineering, including engineering design, structural analysis, RCC structures, etc., under the mentorship of professors.

NON STRUCTURAL

We develop projects related to Hydrology, Irrigation, Transportation, Surveying, Geographic Information Systems, and more under expert guidance from the faculties.
